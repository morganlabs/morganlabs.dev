One of my friends currently works there, and after hearing his stories, it sounds like a job I would enjoy! As much as I do enjoy Subway, I am excited at the idea of this switch at some point in the distant future.

I do think that, at some point in the future, I would like to work in tech. Me and a friend have been passively theorising about a project. I won't give away too much, as we're not sure where this will go, but it's a modern RSS feed reader for mobile and desktop. Chances are, it'll become an open-source project, but we're not sure yet.

I may also look at a pre-existing role in the tech industry. I'm not sure where yet, and that's why I'm taking my time and expanding my abilities in different areas, including learning Rust to get more used to lower-level programming.

I have been interested in working on Apple's design team for quite some time now. I really like the products they design, and I would love to be involved in the process, both in physical products and software, such as iOS and macOS.
